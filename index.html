Bed bugs are notoriously difficult to eliminate and can cause itchy bites and significant distress. Silverfish are primitive insects that feed on carbohydrates, often found in damp, dark areas like bathrooms and basements. While not harmful to humans, they can damage books, paper, and clothing. Silverfish prefer environments with high humidity and are often found in areas where there are moisture issues. Eliminating silverfish requires addressing both the existing infestation and the environmental factors that attract them.
